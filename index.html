Withdrawal Policy

Important: F11 investments have a mandatory 12-month lock-in period from the date of your final investment (Stage 3).

  • No withdrawals allowed during the first 12 months after Stage 3 completion
  • Full withdrawal of your ₹111,111 investment is possible after the 12-month period
  • Ownership benefits continue until withdrawal is processed
  • Withdrawal requests will be processed within 30 days of submission

The 12-month lock-in period ensures stability for the F11 Trust and allows CLUBICLES to execute its growth strategy effectively.
